SimdOrientedFastMersenneTwister

Implements SIMD-Oriented Fast Mersenne Twister. See http://www.math.sci.hiroshima-u.ac.jp/~m-mat/MT/SFMT/index.html for details.

Keeps a random state used in the SIMD-Oriented Fast Mersenne Twister process.
